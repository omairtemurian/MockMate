Problem Title:
    AI-Powered Interview Practice Coach

----------------------------------------------------------------
Context / Background
----------------------------------------------------------------
Job seekers preparing for interviews have two realistic options:
pay a career coach ($100–300/hr), or ask a friend to run a mock
session — which is awkward, inconsistent, and rarely available
on demand. Online prep tools (LeetCode, Glassdoor Q&A, ChatGPT
prompts) give static content but no real conversational practice.

----------------------------------------------------------------
The Problem
----------------------------------------------------------------
There is no low-cost, voice-driven tool that simulates a real
interview conversation — questions tailored to a specific job
description, spoken aloud by an AI, with the candidate answering
by voice — and then delivers scored, actionable feedback.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Evidence / Data
----------------------------------------------------------------
- ~75% of job seekers report that lack of realistic practice is
  their #1 reason for underperforming in interviews
  (LinkedIn Talent Trends, 2023)
- The global interview prep market is valued at $2.4B and growing
  8% YoY, yet dominated by expensive human coaches
- Existing AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini) require users to type
  answers — no voice, no real interview simulation

----------------------------------------------------------------
Impact
----------------------------------------------------------------
Primarily affects:
- Junior and mid-level candidates applying for their first or
  next role
- Career switchers and bootcamp graduates
- International candidates who face language and culture gaps

Secondary impact: hiring managers receive less-prepared
candidates, increasing time-to-hire and cost per hire.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Objectives / Goal
----------------------------------------------------------------
A user can complete a realistic, voice-based mock interview
tailored to any job description, and receive a fully scored
debrief with per-answer feedback — all in under 15 minutes,
at near-zero cost (~$0.001–0.003 per session).
